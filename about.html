Overview

Kampala Insurance Ltd, was established as the first incorporated Insurance Company based in Kampala in August 1980.
We have since spread our wings and sphere of influence as the No. 1 Insurance Company in the region with offices in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Burundi and Rwanda and are recognised as the largest Composite Insurer in East Africa with over 40,000 clients. We are listed in the Nairobi Securities Exchange, Dar e Salaam Stock Exchange and the Nakawa-Kampala, Uganda Securities Exchange having been rated highly on Leadership, Quality and Risk Management and awarded AA- rating in Kenya & Uganda and an A+ in Tanzania
